FINAL OBJECTIVE

Design the world's most advanced Higher Education Financial Intelligence Platform.

A platform where every UK Higher Education Institution can be explored across at least the last decade of financial data, with every annual report transformed into structured, searchable intelligence.

Users should be able to:

explore historical trends,
generate publication-quality comparison graphics,
access interactive financial reports,
understand institutional financial health,
