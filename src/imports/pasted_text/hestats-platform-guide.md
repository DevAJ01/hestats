Final Design Goal

The redesigned HEstats homepage should no longer resemble a marketing website.

Instead, it should feel like the homepage of a £500M financial intelligence company used daily by finance directors, analysts, regulators, policymakers, and institutional investors.

The experience should immediately communicate authority, trust, analytical depth, and credibility. Every visible component should help users analyse UK Higher Education finances rather than persuade them to click a marketing call-to-action.

The benchmark is simple: if someone briefly glances at the interface, they should mistake it for Bloomberg, Palantir Foundry, or a professional institutional research terminal—not an AI-generated SaaS landing page.
