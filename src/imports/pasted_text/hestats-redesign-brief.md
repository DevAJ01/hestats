Final Objective

The final product should not look like a redesigned dashboard.

It should look like a new category of software:

The operating system for UK Higher Education Intelligence.

When a finance director, policymaker, journalist, researcher, or student opens HEStats, they should immediately feel they are using the most comprehensive, trustworthy, and technically sophisticated source of higher education intelligence available anywhere in the world. Every screen should reinforce that HEStats is not merely displaying data—it is making the UK higher education sector understandable.
