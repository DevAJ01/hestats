Expand HESTats into a Complete Higher Education Intelligence Platform

HEStats should evolve beyond financial intelligence into the most comprehensive, data-driven platform for understanding UK Higher Education.

The platform should no longer focus solely on university finances, but instead become the central intelligence platform for every stage of the student and institutional lifecycle.

Every new module should connect with existing financial, research, institutional and historical data to create one interconnected ecosystem.

No statistic should exist in isolation.

The entire platform should behave like an interconnected intelligence graph where users can continuously drill down from national trends to universities, courses, employers, industries and individual metrics.

One recommendation: clearly label indicators that are based on projections or modelled estimates (such as AI impact) versus official published statistics (such as graduate outcomes or enrolment numbers). That distinction will be important for maintaining the platform's credibility and trustworthiness
